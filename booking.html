Standard Operating Procedures (SOP)
General Guidelines:
  • Booking must be made at least 3 days in advance.
  • Only valid University A students/staff are allowed to book.
  • Use the space responsibly and leave it clean after use.
  • Report any equipment damage immediately to management.
  • Food and beverages are NOT allowed inside rooms.
Room Usage Rules:
  • Respect time slots strictly to avoid overlapping bookings.
  • No rearranging of furniture without prior permission.
  • Turn off all electronics (lights, projector, aircon) after use.
  • Maximum occupancy must be followed at all times.
Penalty & Suspension:

Failure to follow SOP may result in temporary suspension of booking privileges and/or disciplinary action.
